About Our Farm House

Nestled in the serene hills of Karjat, just a short drive from Mumbai, Arya Avishkar Resort offers a perfect escape from city life. Our private farmhouse provides eco-friendly stays with lush greenery, organic farms, and breathtaking views.

Our rooms feature balconies, private bathrooms, and mountain/river views. Each room includes air-conditioning, free toiletries, and TV for a comfortable stay.

Whether you seek peace or adventure, Arya Avishkar Resort is your perfect vacation destination with farm tours, yoga, and proximity to Lonavala.
